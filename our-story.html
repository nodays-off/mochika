A Nicaraguan coffee story, brewed over generations

Mochika is more than a brand name. It is our family's journey, our heritage, and the way we share Nicaragua's coffee with the world.

Our Roots

Our family's story begins in the hills of Nicaragua, where coffee trees grew alongside stories, songs, and the rhythms of everyday life. Over the years we learned how to care for the land, harvest each cherry at its peak, and treat coffee as both craft and community.

1960s - 1980s

It started with a small farm

Our grandparents cultivated their first coffee plants in the mountains of Nicaragua. Back then, it was about survival, community, and the simple pride of growing something beautiful. Growing with the next generation

Why we created Mochika

We created Mochika to bring that same care and connection to your daily cup. We believe coffee should be more than a caffeine fix. It should be a moment to pause, connect, and awaken what matters most.

Why Nicaragua?

Nicaragua's coffee-growing regions offer some of the most ideal conditions in the world. High elevations, rich volcanic soil, and cool nights slow ripening, allowing complex flavors to develop naturally.

This is what gives our beans their balanced sweetness, gentle acidity, and the smooth body that makes every cup memorable.
