# Mochika Coffee - Complete Image Generation Prompts for Gemini

## Global Style Guidelines
Apply these to ALL images:
- **Mood**: Warm, welcoming, authentic, not hyper-polished stock
- **Color palette**: Warm browns, soft creams/linen, muted greens, golden yellow, low-saturation powder blue
- **Lighting**: Soft natural light (early morning or late afternoon)
- **People**: Nicaraguan/Central American features, warm expressions, genuine smiles
- **Brand motifs**: Sacuanjoche flower patterns, volcanic mountains, coffee cherries and leaves
